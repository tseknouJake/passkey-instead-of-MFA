Authentication Methods Study
// About this study

Welcome to AuthLab, a university research platform comparing modern authentication methods. This study explores the usability, security, and user experience of four login flows commonly used across the web. Your responses will be used for academic research purposes only.

// Before you begin
📱

The MFA method requires a TOTP authenticator app on your mobile device. Please install Google Authenticator (free on iOS & Android) before starting.

// How to participate
  1. Create an account Register with a username and password. This single account is used across all four authentication methods. We suggest against reusing credentials that you have used previously on other platforms.
  2. Set up all authentication methods After registering, complete the one-time setup for each method: MFA (TOTP), Passkey, and Google login.
  3. Log in using each method, one at a time Return to the login page and authenticate using the first method. After a successful login you will be taken to your dashboard.
  4. Complete the survey After each login, fill in the short questionnaire about that method. Answer based on that specific experience. This takes about 2–3 minutes.
  5. Log out and repeat Log out, then log back in using the next method. Repeat until you have completed all four methods and their surveys.
  6. Optional: Security training A security awareness simulation is available from your dashboard at any time. This is entirely optional and does not affect your survey responses.
